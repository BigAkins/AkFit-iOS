# Logging Notes

Food logging is the most important product loop in AkFit.

## Logging should feel
- fast
- practical
- accurate
- low-friction
- easy to repeat multiple times per day

## Priorities
- fast food search
- useful search results
- clear serving sizes
- easy quantity adjustment
- visible calories and macros
- obvious confirmation after logging

## Food search expectations
Search should work well for:
- specific food variants
- practical foods people actually eat
- portion-based results
- quick repeated logging

## Serving expectations
Serving-size selection should be:
- easy to understand
- realistic
- practical
- fast to edit

Examples:
- 4 oz chicken breast
- 6 oz chicken breast
- 1 cup rice
- 2 eggs
- 1 tbsp peanut butter

## Product rule
AkFit should make it easy for users to answer:
- what did I eat?
- how much did I eat?
- how many calories was that?
- how much protein, carbs, and fat did that add?
- what do I have left today?
